Hair Extensions & Treatments


This project involved creating a sleek, user-friendly website for a hair extensions and treatments business. The design emphasizes elegance, showcasing various hair products and services to attract potential customers. The website includes an easy-to-navigate gallery and a detailed blog section to engage users with hair care tips and trends.

Graphic Designing

This web design project emphasizes clean, modern UI/UX principles to create a visually appealing and user-friendly experience. The focus was on creating an easy-to-navigate interface with seamless functionality across devices. The project includes responsive web elements, interactive components, and visually compelling imagery, providing a cohesive and memorable user experience that aligns with the client’s brand identity. Key aspects such as accessibility, usability, and aesthetics were prioritized to ensure an impactful digital presence.
