Hair Extensions & Treatments

Elegant solutions for beauty businesses

This project involves the creation of a sleek, user-friendly website for a hair extensions and treatments business. Designed to attract potential customers, the website highlights elegance and showcases a range of hair care products and services.

Technologies Used:

HTML5
CSS3
JavaScript
Responsive Design

How It Works:

Step One: Homepage Design

The homepage greets users with a visually appealing layout, featuring high-quality images and an intuitive navigation bar.

Step Two: Product Gallery

A well-organized product gallery displays available hair extensions, helping users browse and choose their desired options with ease.

Step Three: Service Offerings

A dedicated services page highlights all the available treatments, providing detailed descriptions and pricing information for potential clients.
